Description

Heading up the kitchen at Relais Chateaux hotel Le Saint James in Bordeaux is 1 Michelin star chef Nicolas Magie. Before he took up tenancy at Le Saint James, Nicolas worked in some of Frances most celebrated restaurants, including Miramar Biarritz and the Crillon in Paris, alongside chef Christian Constant.

Ingredients

  • carre de cocho iberique
  • 1 rack of lberic pork
  • Salt and Espelette pepper
  • thyme
  • Bay laurel
  • garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Butter
  • Salsify
  • 400g Salsify
  • 400g white poultry stock
  • Espelette pepper
  • salt

Steps

  1. Brown the rack of pork, then add thyme and bay laurel, garlic and butter while basting regularly.
  2. Finish the cooking process in the oven at 180°C. Check temperature. The core of the pork rack should be 62°C once cooked.
  3. Peel the salsify, wash, then cut into small sticks of 8cm of length and cook them in the stock.
  4. Reserve when cooked until lightly crunchy.
  5. Brown the pork bones then add the aromatic dressing
  6. Deglaze with white wine.
  7. Boil down to a syrupy consistence.
  8. Adjust the seasoning to taste and deglaze with lemon juice.
